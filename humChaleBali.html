Day 1 · Jun 5
Arrive & Settle In
Land 8:30 PM → Canggu
🟠 Grab from airport
✈️
Clear immigration — budget 45–60 min at Ngurah Rai. Grab to Canggu ~IDR 100–150k. Ecozy Dijiwa is your first base.
🏨
Check in, freshen up. Tonight is just rest. You've had a long travel day — don't plan anything.
🍜
Late dinner nearby — warungs around Canggu stay open past 10pm. Nasi goreng, fresh coconut, cold Bintang.
💡 Message Bollu Garage or G'Day Rentals on WhatsApp tonight for scooter delivery tomorrow morning.
Day 2 · Jun 6
Canggu + Tanah Lot Sunset
Beach → Temple → Old Man's Bar
🛵 Scooters delivered to villa
🛵
Scooters arrive. Morning orientation ride around Canggu's quieter backstreets — low stakes, good confidence builder.
🏖️
Batu Bolong Beach — watch surfers, walk the sand, lunch at a beachside café.
🛕
Tanah Lot Temple at sunset — 45 min scooter ride. Arrive by 5:30pm. The sea temple on a rock stack at golden hour is exactly as good as it looks. Entry ~IDR 75k pp.
🍺
Old Man's bar — Canggu's iconic beachfront bar. Grab home after drinks — don't scooter at night.
💡 Coastal ride to Tanah Lot at golden hour is stunning — flat road, easy navigation. Perfect Day 2.
Day 3 · Jun 7
Ubud Culture Day
Monkey Forest → Art Market → Kecak Fire Dance
🛵 Scooter to Ubud🟠 Grab to Uluwatu
🐒
Scooter to Ubud (~50 min). Sacred Monkey Forest at 9am — ancient temples, jungle trails, 700+ macaques. ~IDR 120k pp. Don't bring food!
🎨
Ubud art market + Jl. Hanoman — batik, silver, woven goods. Lunch at a warung (~IDR 30k). Pick up a flowing gown here for the swing tomorrow!
💃
Kecak Fire Dance at Uluwatu — take a Grab south. Book the 6pm show in advance. Ramayana fire performance, 100 chanters, cliff-edge Indian Ocean sunset. ~IDR 100–150k pp.
🏨
Check into Alaya Resort Ubud tonight — move base from Canggu to Ubud. Everything for Days 4, 5, 6 is now on your doorstep.
💡 Book Kecak tickets in advance — the 6pm show sells out fast. Arrive at 5pm for good seats.
Day 4 · Jun 8
Rice Terraces + The Swing 📸
Tegallalang → Aloha Ubud Swing
🛵 Short scooter ride from Alaya
🌾
Tegallalang Rice Terraces — leave 7:30am for 8am arrival. UNESCO stepped paddy fields in morning light. Walk the trails. Small donation entry.
📸
Aloha Ubud Swing (10 min away) — THE iconic Bali swing. Bring the gown from yesterday's market. Staff photograph you on your phone — they're genuinely good. Multiple swing heights. Book photo package. ~IDR 200–400k pp.
☕
Jungle café lunch — rice terrace views, infinity pool backdrop, Indonesian food ~IDR 80–120k pp.
🧖
Afternoon at Alaya spa — book a Balinese massage or flower bath for the evening. Well-earned after two full days.
💡 From Alaya, Tegallalang is only 20 min — no more 45-min Canggu slogs. This is why moving your base matters.
Day 5 · Jun 9
Dirt Bike Adventure 🏍️
Jungle trails · River crossings · Luwak coffee
🛵 Short scooter to ATV base
🏍️
ATV Ubud Bali — jungle trails, river crossings, rice fields, cave. ~1.5 hrs. Safety gear, briefing, lunch buffet included. ~IDR 350–550k pp.
☕
Luwak coffee plantation — often included or a short detour. Taste the famous civet coffee. Usually free or very cheap.
🍖
Late lunch: Warung Babi Guling — Bali's famous spit-roast suckling pig. ~IDR 50–80k pp. Go before 2pm — they sell out.
💡 Wear clothes you can get muddy! June dry season = dusty trails, perfect conditions. Bring a change of outfit.
Day 6 · Jun 10
White Water Rafting + Drive to Amed 🌊
Ayung River gorge → Tirta Gangga → Amed
🚗 Private car + driver all day
🚗
Car + driver for the full day (~IDR 600–750k total ÷ 3). Driver holds bags while you raft, then drives east to Amed. Arrange through Alaya the night before.
🌊
Ayung River white water rafting (9am). 2 hrs, Grade 2–3 rapids through dense jungle gorge, past waterfalls and stone carvings. Helmets, life jackets, buffet lunch included. Book Mason Adventures or Bali Adventure Tours. ~IDR 350–450k pp.
🌺
Stop: Tirta Gangga Water Palace en route — royal koi garden, stone fountains, ~IDR 50k entry. Perfect 45-min stop.
🌙
Arrive Amed ~5–6pm. Check into Amed Sari Beach Guesthouse. Fresh grilled fish on the black sand beach.
💡 Rafting in the morning → shower at base → car drives east. The Ayung gorge light is best early too.
Day 7 · Jun 11
Explore Amed + Snorkel
Fishing villages · Jemeluk Bay · Sunrise
🛵 Local scooter rental ~IDR 70k/day
🌅
Sunrise on the beach — Amed faces east. Set alarm for 5:45am. Lombok silhouette, pink sky, complete silence. Worth every second.
🛵
Coastal scooter ride through Jemeluk, Bunutan, Lipah, Selang — quiet roads, traditional outrigger fishing boats on every black sand beach.
🐠
Snorkel at Jemeluk Bay — wade straight in from the beach. Crystal visibility, healthy coral wall, turtles common. Fins + mask rental ~IDR 50k.
🌿
Afternoon: hike the hill behind the village for Agung and Lombok views. Cheapest, freshest fish on the island at local warungs.
💡 Amed roads are the safest, most enjoyable scooter riding of the whole trip — flat coastal road, almost no traffic.
Day 8 · Jun 12
Bungee Jump + Move to Seminyak 🪂
Amed → Lempuyang (optional) → 5GX Kuta
🚗 Private car + driver all day
🌄
Optional: Pura Lempuyang — 7am, 30 min from Amed. Twin temple gates frame Mt. Agung perfectly. One of Bali's best photo moments. ~IDR 50k. Tell driver night before.
🪂
5GX Bali, Kuta — slingshot bungee on Jl. Legian. All three of you ride simultaneously, launching near-vertical. 4.7★, 470+ reviews. ~IDR 400k pp. Book ahead at 5gxbali.com
🏨
Check into Hygge Hotel Seminyak for the final night. Dinner out — the nightlife finale starts tomorrow!
💡 Lempuyang at 7am before crowds = completely different experience. Worth the early alarm if you can manage it.
Day 9 · Jun 13
Potato Head + La Favela Night 🍹
Beach club sunset → Nightlife finale
🛵 Daytime🟠 Grab at night
🛵
Morning: Seminyak boutiques — Jl. Laksamana for local fashion, art prints, silver. Last round of souvenirs.
🏊
Potato Head Beach Club — arrive 4pm for sunset spots. Beachfront pool, great DJs, iconic architecture. Minimum spend ~IDR 150k pp (easily 2 drinks). Stay for the sunset set.
🎶
La Favela, Seminyak — opens 7pm. Six bar areas, Spanish hacienda décor, legendary cocktails. Try the rum brûlée. Dancing until 3–4am. Your nightlife highlight.
💡 Leave scooters at Hygge — always Grab after nightlife. La Favela is a short Grab from all Seminyak stays.
Day 10 · Jun 14
Farewell Morning → Airport ✈️
Depart 2:00 PM · Leave hotel by 11:30am
🟠 Grab to airport
🌸
Slow brunch — Seminyak has great spots open from 8am. After La Favela, no pressure to rush.
🛍️
Last-minute shopping — Seminyak Square or Jl. Kayu Aya. Return scooters (Hygge can call for pickup).
🍛
Farewell lunch by 11am — beachfront warung, fresh grilled fish, one last Bintang.

Grab to Ngurah Rai by 11:30am — 20–30 min. Need 2+ hours for a 2pm international departure. Don't cut it fine!

Getting Around

Smart split — scooters for short scenic rides, car for long hauls, Grab at night

🛵
Scooter — short rides & scenic days
Rent from Bollu Garage or G'Day Rentals in Canggu (~IDR 70–90k/day). Delivered to your door. Best for Ubud backroads, Tegallalang, Canggu-area rides, and Amed's coastal road — all quiet, low-speed, beautiful.
Days 2, 3, 4, 5, 7, 9
🚗
Private car + driver — long hauls
For the Ubud→Amed journey (Day 6) and Amed→Kuta (Day 8). ~IDR 600–750k for a full day, split 3 ways. Your hotel arranges it the night before. Driver waits at each stop — you arrive relaxed, not exhausted.
Days 6 and 8
🟠
Grab — airport, evenings & nightlife
Bali's Grab (like Uber) is cheap, reliable, and essential for nightlife. Never scooter after drinks. Also use it for the Uluwatu evening (Day 3) and airport on Day 1 and Day 14.
Days 1, 3, 9, 14
🚶
Walk — Ubud is made for it
From Alaya Resort, the Monkey Forest, Ubud market, and dozens of restaurants are all walkable. On rafting and ATV days, operators pick you up. Adventure Divers in Amed also collects from your guesthouse.
Days 4, 5, 7

Golden rules

  • Never scooter at night, especially after a drink. Grab is cheap and always available.
  • Fuel scooters yourself at Pertamina stations — avoid touts offering overpriced petrol in bottles near tourist spots.
  • Amed roads are the most relaxed scooter riding of the trip — flat, quiet, coastal. Great for anyone a bit nervous.
  • On car days, WhatsApp your hotel reception the evening before to arrange the driver — don't leave it to the morning.
  • Grab works well in South Bali (Seminyak, Kuta, Canggu) and Ubud. In Amed, your guesthouse owner Wyman can arrange local taxis.
  • Return scooters before your final Grab to the airport on Day 14. Bollu Garage and G'Day both do villa pick-up.

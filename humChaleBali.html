Day 3 · Jun 7
Surf Lesson + Canggu + Bollywood Night 🎶
Double Six Beach → Canggu → Bollywood Club
🛵 Daytime🟠 Grab at night
8:00amSurf lesson — Da Waves Surf School, Double Six Beach. 4.9★, 910+ reviews. 2 hrs, ~IDR 400k pp. Boards, rash guards, life jackets provided. Instructors are patient with beginners — decide on the day how far you push it

💡 Life jackets are available at Da Waves — no pressure to go beyond your comfort level. Even paddling and attempting to stand is genuinely fun. Both club and villa are in Seminyak — short Grab home.
Day 4 · Jun 8
🕺 La Favela Night — The Big One
Slow morning → Rooftop cocktails → La Favela
🛵 Daytime🟠 Grab at night
10:00amSlow start — brunch at Livingstone Café, Jl. Petitenget (4.8★, 13,500+ reviews, 5 min walk). Creamy tiramisu latte, chicken pita, fresh pastries

Move to Ubud + Culture Day
Return scooters → Grab to Ubud → Monkey Forest → Kecak
🟠 Grab to Ubud🟠 Grab to Uluwatu

Ivy Cafe + Rice Terraces + The Swing + Ridge Walk 📸
Tegallalang → Aloha Swing → Campuhan → Bridges Dinner
🛵 Short rides from Alaya

💡 From Alaya, Tegallalang is only 20 min — vs 50+ from Seminyak. This is the whole reason the Ubud base matters. The ridge walk at 5pm catches the best golden hour light of the day.

Spa & Relaxation

One full dedicated spa day (Day 5, Seminyak) — two strong options right near La Boutique

Seminyak — Day 5 full spa day · post La Favela recovery

📍 Jl. Petitenget · Across from La Boutique
Sundari Wellness
Full-service spa · 9am–10pm daily
Rating4.9★ · 6,070 reviews
Best packageSundari Shakti Ritual (3.5 hrs)
Price range~IDR 300–700k pp
Feet scrub + wash → full Balinese massage → body scrub → optional collagen facial. Reviewers describe it as transformative. Professional, spiritual atmosphere. Literally across the road from La Boutique — just walk over. Book via WhatsApp 2 days in advance, mention group of 3.
📍 Jl. Drupadi · 5 min scooter from La Boutique
Eden Spa & Beauty
Boutique spa · Opens 10:30am
Rating5.0★ · 3,956 reviews
Best packageSignature massage + Facial (2.5 hrs)
Price range~IDR 250–500k pp
Welcome fruit + mineral water → choose essential oil scent → Balinese massage → homemade hibiscus tea. Small, intimate, personal — feels like being looked after by family. Perfect for three going together. Great value for the quality.
📍 Jl. Petitenget · Inside Alila Hotel
Spa Alila Seminyak
Luxury hotel spa · 9am–9pm daily
Rating5.0★ · 665 reviews
Best packageWellness Escape 180 min
Price range~IDR 500k–1M pp
Balinese massage + avocado hair spa + lulur body scrub + flower-filled bubble bath at the end. The splurge option — if you want to fully indulge, this is it. Beautiful resort facilities. Chocolate coconut scrub + steam combo is a standout.
Booking tip: WhatsApp all spas 2–3 days in advance. Mention you're a group of 3 and ask for a private room or adjoining rooms. Almost all Bali spas accommodate groups happily — it's a very common request.

Golden rules

  • Never scooter at night or after any drinks. Grab is ~IDR 20–40k for short hops in Seminyak and Ubud — always available.
  • Never load luggage onto scooters for a long journey. Day 6 Seminyak→Ubud is a Grab with bags in the boot. Always.
  • Return Seminyak scooters Day 6 morning before leaving — Bollu Garage and G'Day both do villa pick-up.
  • Rent fresh scooters in Ubud from Alaya — only need them Days 7 & 8, then you're done with scooters.
  • Amed coastal roads are the safest, most relaxed scooter riding of the whole trip — flat, almost no traffic, beautiful.
  • Arrange car + driver the evening before through your hotel — never leave it to the morning of a long travel day.
  • In Amed, ask Wyman at the guesthouse to arrange the airport car — he knows reliable local drivers and the exact timing needed.
  • Fuel scooters at Pertamina stations — avoid touts selling petrol in bottles near tourist spots.

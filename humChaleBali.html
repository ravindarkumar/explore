Golden rules

  • Never scooter at night or after drinks. Grab is cheap and always available in Seminyak and Ubud.
  • Never load luggage onto scooters for a long journey. Day 6 Seminyak→Ubud is a Grab with bags in the boot.
  • Scooters are essential at Villa Viandra — the villa is 15 min from Ubud town. Rent them as soon as you check in Day 6.
  • Return Seminyak scooters Day 3 evening — Bollu Garage does villa pickup. No scooters Days 4 or 5.
  • Return Ubud scooters Day 8 after the Ridge Walk before Bridges dinner.
  • Rafting operator (Mason Adventures) picks up from Villa Viandra — no car needed Day 9.
  • Arrange the airport car through Villa Viandra the evening before Day 10 — don't leave it to the morning.
  • Fuel scooters at Pertamina stations — avoid touts selling petrol in bottles near tourist spots.
